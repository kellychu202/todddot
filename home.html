Welcome to Hop On!

Bored out of your mind in quarantine? Stuck at home with the same two hobbies you've had since middle school?

Hop On is a website that creates a personalized calendar for your needs, fit with tailored recommendations of different hobbies to try. Make use of our daily scheduler where you can drag & drop our specially curated quarantine-friendly activities, or use the randomizer to have us do all the work and come up with a schedule ourselves!
